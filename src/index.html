Welcome to The 5th ICLLLE 2025!

We are proud to present this international conference, organized by the Faculty of Arts and Education, Universitas Teknokrat Indonesia. This year marks the fifth ICLLLE, following the success of the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th conferences. With the theme “Digital Frontiers in Language, Literary Studies, and Pedagogy,” the conference explores a wide range of innovative sub-themes, from artificial intelligence in language to contemporary digital culture. Our goal is to facilitate productive academic dialogue, enrich teaching methodologies, and provide publication opportunities for researchers. Hosted in collaboration not only with regional partners but also with international institutions, ICLLLE 2025 warmly welcomes participants from across the globe.We wish you insightful discussions, fruitful collaborations, and inspiring explorations into the digital frontiers of the social sciences and humanities!
